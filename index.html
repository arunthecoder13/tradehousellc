Tradehouse Gold Commitment to Responsible Practices

At Tradehouse Gold, we are dedicated to upholding the highest business and ethical standards in all our operations. We understand the importance of responsible gold production and are committed to conducting our work with integrity and diligence.


Key Elements of Our Approach:
  • Compliance Measures: We have implemented robust compliance measures to ensure adherence to ethical standards throughout our supply chain. Dedicated officers continuously monitor and mitigate risks to uphold our commitment to responsible business conduct.
  • Due Diligence Framework: Our due diligence framework is based on comprehensive risk assessment and follows guidance issued by respected organizations including:
    • OECD (Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development)
    • LBMA (London Bullion Market Association)
    • RMI (Responsible Mineral Initiative)
    • DMCC (Dubai Multi Commodities Center)

Our Goal:

Our ultimate goal is to produce gold responsibly, contributing to sustainable practices in the gold industry while maintaining the trust and confidence of our stakeholders.

By aligning with internationally recognized standards and continuously improving our practices, we demonstrate our commitment to ethical conduct and environmental stewardship.

At Tradehouse Gold, responsible business conduct is not just a commitment, but a fundamental principle that guides our operations every day.
